Sorcery

The use of power gained from the assistance or control of evil spirits especially for divining. A person who can perform sorcery is called a sorcerer (which can be used for any gender, but typically refers to a man) or a sorceress (which refers to a woman). The words sorcery implies great power—and often great skill and command of such power.
